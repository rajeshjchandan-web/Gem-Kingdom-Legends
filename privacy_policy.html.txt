Privacy Policy for Gem Kingdom Legends

Effective Date: 2026-06-03

Gem Kingdom Legends stores gameplay progress locally on your device, including coins, levels, achievements, rewards, and settings.

The Android version may display ads, including rewarded ads, through third-party advertising networks such as Google AdMob. These networks may collect device or advertising identifiers according to their own privacy policies.

The game does not require account login. Progress is stored locally on your device. Removing the app may remove saved progress.

For privacy questions, contact the app developer through the contact details listed on the Google Play Store page.
